## Getting Started with Pinocchio

First things first, download the software called Pinocchio from their [official website](https://pinokio.computer/). It's user-friendly and perfect for those who aren't keen on diving deep into technical details or running complex scripts. 

For a visual guide, you can watch the [Changing Faces with AI: An Easy Guide to Face Swapping](https://youtu.be/rMAQcu-LFv4?si=EmKJda3Rz1P2Vtgj) video i made on YouTube.

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Download Pinocchio**: Go to the [Pinocchio website](https://pinokio.computer/) and download the software. Follow the setup instructions provided.

2. **Launch the App**: Once installed, open Pinocchio. You'll see a feature called "Face Fusion". This is your go-to tool for face swapping.

3. **Face Fusion**: In the Pinocchio app, navigate to the 'Featured' section and locate Face Fusion. Download it, and the app will take care of the rest, including running the necessary Python scripts.

4. **Web UI**: After installation, Pinocchio will present a web-based user interface. It might seem a bit daunting at first, but hang in there! 

### Tips for Better Results

- **Experiment**: Play around with the settings to see what works best for your specific needs.
